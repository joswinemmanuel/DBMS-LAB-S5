Step 1 : Right click on table to be exported
	 to an Excel file.

Step 2 : Click Export.

Step 3 : Select Format xlsx.

Step 4 : Select Save As Separate files.

Step 5 : Give file name in Data Worksheet Name.

Step 6 : Click Next.

Step 7 : Click Finish.
	 The data will be exported from the table to xlsx file.
